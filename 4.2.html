Key Learning Points

✦ Negotiations are the heart of MUN — happen everywhere, require preparation, listening, and smart compromise.

✦ Drafting Resolutions needs structure (preamble + operative clauses), precision, and majority appeal.

✦ Amendments let you shape the final document — friendly ones are easy, unfriendly ones need votes.

These skills turn individual ideas into collective solutions — the true power of Model UN diplomacy.
