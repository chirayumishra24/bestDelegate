Forged Coalition

Manage Internal Conflicts

Address disagreements quickly & respectfully • seek compromise to keep the coalition strong & united.

Coalition building turns individual voices into unstoppable blocs — identify, connect, trust, strategize, and sustain to lead the committee.

Benefits

 

Coalition Power Boost

Strong coalitions enhance voting power, influence debates, and dramatically increase resolution success.

 

Skill Multiplier

Teamwork builds essential diplomatic skills — negotiation, leadership, collaboration — that are valuable in MUN and real-world diplomacy.

 
Effective coalition building and teamwork involve strategic planning, strong relationships, open communication, conflict resolution, and inclusive collaboration to succeed in Model UN.

Key Learning Points

Coalition Building starts with research — identify allies early, form blocs, and develop shared strategies.

Teamwork means trust, inclusion, compromise, and communication — every member’s voice matters.

Strong alliances turn individual efforts into collective power — the key to passing resolutions and winning awards.
