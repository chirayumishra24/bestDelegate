What is the Crisis Committee Challenge?

This activity places you in the most intense format of Model UN: Crisis Committees.

You will experience:

  • Real-time crisis updates that force instant decision-making
  • Individual crisis notes & directives that only affect your country
  • Balancing public speeches with private back-channel diplomacy
  • Rapid alliance shifts, betrayals, and power plays
  • Writing urgent directives under time pressure

This simulation helps you develop crisis management, strategic thinking, adaptability, and calm leadership under chaos — skills that separate top delegates from the rest.

⚡

Objective of This Challenge

Survive multiple crisis injections, maintain control of your country's narrative, form temporary coalitions, and emerge as the most influential delegate by the end of the session.

Key Takeaways from the Crisis Challenge

✦ Crisis committees reward speed, creativity, and calm under pressure — not just preparation.

✦ Private directives allow bold moves that public speeches cannot achieve.

✦ Temporary alliances are essential — but trust is fragile and betrayal is common.

✦ Strong crisis performance dramatically boosts your overall award chances.

The delegates who thrive in crisis are the ones who adapt fastest and lead decisively when everyone else is reacting.
